# Demo Readiness for James

## Demo Objective

Show that MJ understands enough of HighLevel CRM to explain how an AI Orchestrator / Business Systems Investigator could help diagnose CRM setup, automation, workflow, pipeline, and integration issues.

## Minimum Confidence Required

Before a controlled demo or deeper discussion, MJ should be able to explain:

- What HighLevel Contacts are used for
- How Tags and Custom Fields support Contacts
- How Opportunities and Pipelines work
- How Workflows trigger and perform actions
- How Forms capture leads
- How Calendars and Appointments connect to workflows
- How Conversations show email/SMS/activity history
- How Snapshots are used as reusable setup templates
- Where integrations and webhooks can fail
- How an unfamiliar admin would investigate a broken process
